Getting Your Team Set Up is Easy

Follow these simple steps to get started in minutes.

Step 1: Create Your Admin Account & Team

Click the "Sign Up" button. You'll enter your name, email, password, and the name for your new team. This automatically makes you the administrator for that team.

Step 2: Customize Your Profile (Optional)

Once you're logged into your new dashboard, you can visit the "Profile" tab to change your name or update your team's name. This name will be shown to your employees when they join.

Step 3: Copy Your Team's Invitation Link

On your main dashboard, you'll find your team's unique and secure invitation link. Click the "Copy Link" button to copy it to your clipboard.

Step 4: Send the Link to Your Employees

Paste the invitation link into an email, text message, or your company's chat application and send it to your employees. That's it! They can now clock in and out, and you'll see their activity on your dashboard in real-time.
