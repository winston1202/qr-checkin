Powerful Features for Modern Teams

Everything you need for simple and secure time tracking.

Geolocation Verification

Ensure employees are on-site with a secure geofence. The system verifies their location before every clock-in and clock-out.

Real-Time Admin Dashboard

See who's currently clocked in, view historical logs, and manage your team, all from one simple dashboard.

Frictionless for Employees

No accounts or passwords needed for employees to clock in. They just click their invitation link once and are ready to go.

Self-Serve Team Management

Admins (bosses) can create their team, customize its profile, and manage their own users without developer intervention.

Optional Employee Accounts

Employees have the option to create a password to view their own time clock history, promoting transparency.

Data Export

Admins can filter and export time logs as a CSV file, perfect for payroll processing or archiving.
