An honest tool for an honest day's work.

QrCheckin was built to solve a real-world problem with a simple, secure, and straightforward solution.

Our Story

This project began with a simple request. A local business manager, a friend of my mother's, was facing a common but frustrating challenge: managing employee time logs accurately. The existing system was cumbersome, and there were growing concerns about the integrity of clock-in and clock-out times. It was clear they needed a tool that was not only easy for employees to use but also provided accountability for management.

The Problem We Solve

For many businesses, trust is the foundation of their team. But manual time tracking or honor-based systems can lead to inaccuracies, disputes, and what the manager politely called "deceitfulness." These small discrepancies add up, impacting payroll, project costs, and overall team morale. They needed a system that was fair, transparent, and removed the guesswork.

A One-Person Mission

As a single developer, I took on this challenge personally. I believe that powerful tools don't need to be complicated. Every feature in QrCheckin, from the simple employee check-in to the secure geofence verification, was designed with one goal in mind: to be the simplest, most reliable time tracking tool a small business can use.
