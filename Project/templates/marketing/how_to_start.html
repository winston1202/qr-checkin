Getting Your Team Set Up is Easy

Start tracking time in just a few minutes.

Step 1: Choose Your Plan & Create an Account

Click "Sign Up" on our pricing page. The Free plan is perfect for getting started. You'll enter your name, email, password, and a name for your team. This makes you the first administrator.

Step 2: Copy Your Team's Invitation Link

From your new admin dashboard, you'll find your team's unique and secure invitation link. Click the "Copy Link" button.

Step 3: Send the Link to Your Employees

Paste the link into an email, text, or chat and send it to your employees. As soon as they click the link and enter their name, they can clock in, and you'll see their activity on your dashboard in real-time.
