Getting Your Team Set Up is Easy

Step 1: Create Your Admin Account & Team

Click "Sign Up". You'll enter your name, email, password, and a name for your team. This makes you the first administrator for that team.

Step 2: Copy Your Team's Invitation Link

From your new dashboard, you'll find your team's unique and secure invitation link. Click the "Copy Link" button.

Step 3: Send the Link to Your Employees

Paste the link into an email, text, or chat and send it to your employees. They can now clock in, and you'll see their activity on your dashboard.
