Having an Issue?

If you've found a bug or have a problem not answered below, please let us know! Provide as much detail as possible, including screenshots and the steps you took.

Report an Issue

For Admins (Team Managers)

How do I invite an employee to my team? ▶
Log in to your Admin Dashboard. On the main page, you will see a "Team Invitation Link". Click the "Copy Link" button and send this URL to your employees. This is a "smart link"â€”new employees will be guided through setup, and existing employees will be taken directly to their clock-in workflow.
An employee got a new phone. How do they clock in? ▶
Go to the "Users" tab on your dashboard. Find the employee in the list and click the "Clear Token" button. This unlinks their name from their old device. They can then click the original invitation link on their new device to register it to their name. All their past time logs will be preserved.
How do I upgrade to the Pro plan? ▶
While logged in, you will see an "Upgrade to Pro" button in the header of your admin dashboard. You can also visit the public Pricing page. Clicking the upgrade button will take you to a secure Stripe page to complete the subscription.
How do I manage my subscription or cancel my plan? ▶
If you are on the Pro plan, a "Manage" link will appear in the header of your admin dashboard. Clicking this will take you to a secure Stripe Customer Portal where you can update your payment method, view invoices, or cancel your subscription at any time.
How do I make another user an Admin? (Pro Feature) ▶
First, your team must be on the Pro plan. Second, the employee must have created an account with an email and password. Once both conditions are met, go to the "Users" tab, find the user you want to promote, and the "Promote" button will be active.
What is the Security Audit Log for? (Pro Feature) ▶
The Audit Log, available on the Pro plan, provides extra security by tracking important events. It records every time an employee fails a geofence check (tries to clock in while too far away) and every time someone tries to clock in with a name that doesn't match the registered user for that device.

For Employees

How do I clock in? ▶
Your manager will send you a unique "Team Invitation Link". For your first time, you will click this link and enter your name. For all future clock-ins, you can just reuse the same link or visit the main website URL, and the system will automatically recognize your device.
The screen says my device is registered to someone else. What do I do? ▶
This is a security feature. It means the phone or computer you are using is already linked to another employee's name. You must ask your manager to find that employee's name on the "Users" tab and click "Clear Token" for them. This will free up the device for you to use.
How can I see my past hours? ▶
After you clock in or out, the success page will have a "Create My Account" button. This allows you to create a free, optional account with an email and password. Once you have an account, you can log in at any time to view your personal dashboard and see a full history of all your time entries.
