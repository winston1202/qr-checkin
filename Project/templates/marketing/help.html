Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use the time clock system.

Having an Issue?

If you've found a bug or have a problem not answered below, please let us know! Provide as much detail as possible, including screenshots and the steps you took.

Report an Issue

For Admins (Team Managers)

How do I invite an employee to my team? ▶
Log in to your Admin Dashboard. On the main page, you will see a "Team Invitation Link". Click the "Copy Link" button and send this URL to your employees via email, text, or another messaging service.
An employee got a new phone. How do they clock in? ▶
Go to the "Users" tab on your dashboard. Find the employee in the list and click the "Clear Token" button. This will free up their name. They can then click the original invitation link on their new device to register it to their name. All their past time logs will be preserved.
How do I make another user an Admin? ▶
Go to the "Users" tab. Find the user you want to promote and click the "Promote to Admin" button. They will now have access to the admin dashboard. Note: They will need to create an account with a password first if they haven't already.
What does the "Location Verification" setting do? ▶
When this setting is ON, employees must be within the 500-foot geofence of your building to clock in or out. When it is OFF, they can clock in from anywhere. You can toggle this for your team at any time from the "Settings" tab.

For Employees

How do I clock in for the first time? ▶
You must use the unique invitation link sent to you by your manager. Click the link, enter your full name, and follow the on-screen instructions.
How do I clock in on other days? ▶
After you join a team, your device is "remembered". You can simply go to the main website URL. The system will recognize your device and automatically start the clock-in or clock-out process for you.
